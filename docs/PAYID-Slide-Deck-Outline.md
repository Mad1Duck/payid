# PAY.ID — Slide Deck Outline (5–7 Slides)

## Slide 1 — Title
PAY.ID
Programmable Payment Identity Protocol

## Slide 2 — Problem
Payment policy is hard-coded everywhere.
This causes high cost, rigidity, and poor auditability.

## Slide 3 — Solution
Separate decision from execution.
Off-chain rule evaluation.
On-chain verifiable proof.

## Slide 4 — Architecture
Context → Rule Engine → Decision Proof → Verifier

## Slide 5 — Why It’s Different
- Non-custodial
- Deterministic
- ERC-4337 ready
- Bank / QRIS extensible

## Slide 6 — Impact
Safer payments.
Better UX.
Auditable policy.

## Slide 7 — Ask
Funding for protocol R&D and ecosystem tooling.
